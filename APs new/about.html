About APS Rewa University

Founded in 2000, APS Rewa University has been a beacon of excellence in education for over two decades. Our institution is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation in various fields of engineering and technology.

Located in the heart of Rewa, Madhya Pradesh, our campus spans over 50 acres of lush greenery, providing an ideal environment for learning and research. We take pride in our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.

Our university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, with a focus on practical learning and industry exposure. We believe in nurturing not just technical skills but also leadership qualities and ethical values in our students.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning, known for excellence in education, research, and innovation, producing leaders who will shape the future of technology and society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for professional success and social responsibility, while fostering innovation and research in emerging technologies.
